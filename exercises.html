Full body exercises


Back exercises

Back consists of mainly lats , upper/mid/lower back and traps

    Overall back exercises

    1. Deadlifts
    2. Bent-Over Barbell Rows (overhand grip)
    3. T-bar rows

    Lats

    1. Lat-Focused Cable Row (elbows tucked in)
    2. Chin-Ups
    3. Lat Pulldowns
    4. Pull-Ups

    Upper/mid back

    Note : perform upper and mid back exercises in elbows out stance

    1. Cable Row
    2. Wide Grip Chest Supported Machine Rows
    3. Dumbbell Chest Supported Row
    4. Inverted rows(at last)

    Lower back

    1. Back extensions

    Lower traps

    1. Inverted Y rows (weighted or unweighted)

Bicep exercises

Bicep mainly consists of long head , short head and brachiallis

    Long head

    1. Incline Dumbbell Curls
    2. Cable curl (with bar)

    Short head

    1. Concentration Curls (very important)

    Brachiallis

    1. Dumbbell Hammer Curls

Chest exercises

Chest mainly consists of upper , mid and lower chest

    Upper chest

    1. 15 Degree Incline Dumbbell Press
    2. 30 Degree Incline Dumbbell Press
    3. Barbell Bench Press
    4. Seated Cable Fly
    5. Incline Machine Press
    6. Flat Dumbbell Press

    Mid chest

    1. Seated Cable Fly
    2. Slight Decline Dumbbell Press (not with bench decline , instead place a plate on the other end of the bench to decline)
    3. 15 Degree Incline Dumbbell Press
    4. Pec Deck Machine
    5. Flat Dumbbell Press
    6. High To Low Cable Fly

    Lower chest

    1. Seated Cable Fly
    2. Slight Decline Dumbbell Press (not with bench decline , instead place a plate on the other end of the bench to decline)
    3. Flat Dumbbell Press
    4. Pec Deck Machine
    5. High To Low Cable Fly
    6. Barbell Bench Press

Tricep Exercises

Tricep mainly consists of long , lateral and medial head.

    Long Head

    1. Triangle Pushups
    2. Incline Dumbbell Overhead Extensions
    3. Incline Tricep Kickbacks

    Lateral Head

    1. Triangle Pushups
    2. Bench Dips
    3. Bar Pushdowns

    Medial Head

    1. Triangle Pushups
    2. V-Rope Pushdowns

Shoulder Exercises

Shoulder mainly consists of front , side and rear delts

    Front Delts

    1. Seated Dumbbell Shoulder Press
    2. Seated Barbell Shoulder Press

    Side Delts

    1. Dumbbell Lateral Raise
    2. Lying Incline Lateral Raise
    3. Cable Lateral Raise

    Rear Delts

    1. Cable Rear Delt Flyes(Double Arm)
    2. Cable Rear Delt Flyes(Single Arm)
    3. Reverse Pec Deck Machine
    4. Cable Rear Delt Rows

Leg Exercises

Leg mainly consists of quads , glutes , calfs and hamsmtrings

    Quads

    1. Back Squats OR Front Squats
    2. Stationery Split Squats (better) or Forward Lunges
    3. Leg extensions

    Glutes

    1. Barbell Hip Thrusts
    2. Side Raises
    3. Reverse Lunges

    Calfs

    1. Standing Calf Raises
    2. Seated Calf Raises

    Hamstrings

    1. Leg Curls (more than enough)
    2. Glute Hamstring Raises
